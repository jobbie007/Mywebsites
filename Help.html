Help infomation

If password is invalid make sure your password has at least:
  • 1 character
  • 1 capital letter
  • 1 number
  • 1 symbol
If number is invalid make sure your number has 10 numbers.
If email is invalid make sure that your email has a @ symbol and a '.'
To register for a course click the names of the courses on the top right hand corner and click register.
If you have made a payment and the amount owned is not updating please restart the application
